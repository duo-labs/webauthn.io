Matthew Miller

Matthew Miller is a Tech Lead on the Cisco Secure team helping to build a user-centric Duo Passwordless experience.

In addition to representing Cisco in two WebAuthn Working Groups, and being a co-chair of the WebAuthn Adoption Community Group, Matthew created and maintains two WebAuthn libraries including py_webauthn and SimpleWebAuthn.

Got a question? Get in touch over at the duo-labs/webauthn.io repo.
